Scoring Methodology
How the recommendation engine ranks models — click to expand

Scoring Formula

final_score = coding×wc + math×wm + reasoning×wr + chat×wch

Each model's raw benchmark score in a category is multiplied by that category's normalized weight, then all four contributions are summed. Weights are normalized so they always sum to 100%, regardless of the values you enter.
